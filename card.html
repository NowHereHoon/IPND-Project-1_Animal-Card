Dolphin

It has been proved by scientists that dolphins give themselves names. They develop their own individual whistles and they recognize theirs and other dolphins’ names.

  • Scientific Name: Delphinus delphis
  • Average Length: 3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 29 years
  • Habitat: Ocean or Brackish waters along coastlines.

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals that live in water but breathe through a blowhole on the top of their head. They are remarkable creatures that have developed a form of sonar called echolocation to hunt and prey.
